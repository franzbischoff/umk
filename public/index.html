Uyamak

A online simulation platform for the mathematical models

How to simulate the above graph


Uyamak is a fully online OS independent simulation platform which runs on your browser. Similar to Simulink or LabVIEW, it uses visual programming to generate the mathematical models.

Uyamak simulates both static and dynamic mathematical models. Its visual programming is intuitive and self-explanatory. Hence, it is a perfect tool for the students, teacher, and researchers.

Uyamak uses your computer's processor to generate a model hence you don't have to wait for the simulation results. It loads the library on the fly, which means you don't have to spare your resources to download the whole library.

Similar to Simulink, Uyamak is also compatible with matrices. It lets the users develop the complex algorithms with a little effort.

Distinguished features

Here is the list of some of the important features that you may be interested in.

No download

You don't have to download any software. The program is light weight and hence uses very little of your internet data.

Instant results

Uyamak gathers just the right amount of resources to run your model and it runs on your PC. The results are available to you instantly

No coding

Uyamak lets you numerically solve a mathematical problem by connecting blocks without the need of learning any programming language.
